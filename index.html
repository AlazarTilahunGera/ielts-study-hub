📝 Writing Practice Prompt

Prompt: Some people believe that governments should invest more money in public services like health and education. Others believe that more money should be spent on infrastructure such as roads and bridges. Discuss both views and give your opinion.

📘 Grammar Focus: Linking Words

Use these linking words in your essays:

  • Add ideas: also, in addition, moreover
  • Contrast: however, although, on the other hand
  • Examples: for example, such as, for instance
  • Cause/Effect: because, therefore, as a result

Practice:

  1. I like studying English. ________, I find it very useful for my future.
  2. Some people prefer working from home. ________, others enjoy being in an office.

📚 Academic Vocabulary (Week 1)

  • allocate – to set aside for a purpose (e.g. money)
  • infrastructure – physical systems like roads and bridges
  • priority – something more important than others
  • essential – absolutely necessary or very important
  • improve – to make something better
